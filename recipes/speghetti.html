Spaghetti

I haven't had this in a while, but it's a good dish when you are too lazy to cook

Ingredents

  • Pasta
  • Store-bought Spaghetti Sauce

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a pot with medium heat
  2. Add pasta into the pot
  3. Read the label behind the package and follow their time instruction. It can be from 5 mins to 10 mins
  4. After the pasta has cooked, heat the store-bought sauce onto the pan
  5. After 1 min, add cooked pasta onto the pan
  6. mix it up for 1 min and turn off the heat
  7. plate your dish and enjoy !
