Fried Egg

It's a simple dish, really. But the taste of this dish is still my favorite dish of all time. I love to pair it with cooked rice and soy sauces.

Ingredients

  • Eggs (It can be duck or chicken egg)
  • Soy sauces
  • Chili Flakes

Steps

  1. Heat your pan till it's hot that you can see smoke
  2. Add oil onto your pan
  3. Crack your egg onto the pan
  4. Wait for 30 sec
  5. Take your egg out of your pan
  6. Add soy sauces and chill flakes on your egg

And well done ! You can enjoy your meal to your heart content.

NOTE

If you like runny egg, you should take it out out your pan in 15sec
